<h3>Combinations</h3>

<p>For set = <code>[1, 2, 3, 4, 5]</code> and <code>size = 3</code> we get:</p>

<p><code>(0, 1, 2)</code></p>
<p><code>(0, 1, 3)</code></p>
<p><code>(0, 1, 4)</code></p>
<p><code>(0, 2, 3)</code></p>
<p><code>(0, 2, 4)</code></p>
<p><code>(0, 3, 4)</code></p>
<p><code>(1, 2, 3)</code></p>
<p><code>(1, 2, 4)</code></p>
<p><code>(1, 3, 4)</code></p>
<p><code>(2, 3, 4)</code></p>
